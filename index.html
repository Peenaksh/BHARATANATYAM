DANCE FORM OF INDIA

BHARATNATYAM

Bharatanatyam the oldest classical dance heritage of India is regarded as mother of many Indian classical dance forms and it originated in TamilNadu . It is principally common in the South Indian state of Tamil Nadu and its province; and is nearly 2,000 years old..Bharatanatyam dance is known to be ekaharya, where one dancer takes on many roles in a single performance . In the early 19th century, the famous Tanjore Quartette,under the patronage of Raja Serfoji are said to have been responsible for the repertoire of Bharatnatyam dance as we see it today.
